    About Us

    At Nichewavetech, we believe that great businesses are built on smart systems. We specialize in delivering cutting-edge ERP solutions that empower organizations to streamline operations, boost productivity, and make data-driven decisions with confidence.


    Founded in 2025 and operating from Hyderabad, India with a vision to simplify complexity, we specialize in implementation, customization, upgradation using Infor OS, Infor EPR LN, M3, Siteline, WMS and, Factory Track. Whether you're a growing startup or an established enterprise, our scalable solutions adapt to your needs and evolve with your goals. Our solutions fulfil discreet manufacturing, retail domains in Global markets.


    With total of 110 years of experience in ERP Infor ecosystem, we have built a reputation for excellence, and customer satisfaction.


    We’re not just software providers - we’re strategic partners in your digital transformation journey. With innovation at our core and excellence in our delivery, we help businesses unlock their full potential.


    Our Mission: Committed to delivering tailored solutions, expert guidance, and unwavering support - transforming complex systems into powerful engines of efficiency and innovation.


    Our Vision: To be one of the most trusted ERP implementation partners globally, known for our deep expertise, agile delivery, and transformative impact on organizations.
